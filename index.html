About Me

Ebubechukwu Ozurumba aka Bube, is a results-driven Engineer recognized for designing smarter systems, optimizing manufacturing operations, and turning complex technical challenges into efficient, scalable solutions.

He brings strong expertise in manufacturing engineering, process improvement, CAD design, CNC processes, lean methodologies, and data-driven optimization. His work spans cycle-time reduction, process standardization, scrap and waste minimization, and the design of tooling, fixtures, and other functional engineering solutions.

Bube approaches engineering with a unique blend of analytical discipline and creative problem-solving. He is passionate about Industry 4.0, digital workflows, and exploring how AI can transform modern manufacturing and process automation.

He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and a Master’s degree in Manufacturing Engineering Technology. He is also a certified Lean Six Sigma Green Belt with additional training in Operational Excellence.
